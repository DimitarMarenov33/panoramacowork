Questions

Frequently asked

Is the internet actually reliable?
Two independent providers, automatic failover, UPS backup, and live speed tests published on this page. And if it fails for 15+ minutes during your day — the day is free. We put money behind the answer.
How do I get in?
After you book, you get a personal 6-digit door code by email. Type it on the keypad at the entrance — it's active from the start date of your booking.
Can I take video calls?
Yes — that's what the soundproof phone booths are for. Book one in the app or just step in when free.
Do I need a contract?
No. Day and week passes are one-off. Monthly plans renew automatically but cancel any month with zero notice.
Can my company pay?
Yes — we invoice companies directly (EU VAT invoices). The Workation Package is one email and one invoice for the whole team.
What happens in winter?
We stay open — with a €99/month local plan and a €990 annual plan (pay 10 months, get 2 free). Leaving in September? The Reserved Seat plan (€19/month) keeps your mail address, community access, 2 day passes monthly, and your locked summer price for next year.
Where exactly are you?
Central Nesebar — minutes from the old town, 200m from the beach. Exact address and map coming with the opening announcement.
